What income thresholds enable migration?

Income can be a strong determinant of migration. Higher income households have more resources and mobility to take on this challenge. They may have more savings to finance their relocation, the means to access safe and reliable migration routes, and better access to information about job opportunities and housing abroad. Individuals may be more likely to have specialized skills or education that make them attractive to employers in other regions.
